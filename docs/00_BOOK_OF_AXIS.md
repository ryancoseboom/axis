# The Book of Axis

## Mission

Axis is a personal reasoning engine that helps people become someone
they deeply respect by helping them make meaningful progress across the
pursuits that matter most.

## Why Axis Exists

People rarely fail because they lack ambition. They drift because the
urgent replaces the important. Axis exists to prevent drift and help
people spend their lives well.

## The North Star

Optimize **self-respect**, not productivity.

Self-respect grows from repeatedly acting in accordance with one's
principles.

## The Thursday Test

Version Zero succeeds if Axis consistently creates a better ordinary
Thursday than the user would have created alone.

## Constitution

1.  Today is the product.
2.  Axis never creates anxiety.
3.  Progress over activity.
4.  Identity over tasks.
5.  Self-respect is the north star.
6.  The user remains in control.
7.  Every recommendation has evidence.
8.  Momentum over perfection.
9.  Reality always wins.
10. Today is computed, not stored.
11. Reduce thinking.
12. Never build infrastructure until it has a user.

## Final Principle

Build the smallest thing that genuinely helps someone spend today well.
